Local Guppy Accepted into Saginaw Valley State University!

We here at Lake12 News asked her parents how they felt.

They replied with this: "We are so proud of and exited for her. Now she can finaly pursue her dream to be become a nuse!"

She will be the first guppy to ever attend the university this fall.

Locals gathered early this week to wish her well before she left town for SVSU.

This and more at 10 o'clock this evening.
